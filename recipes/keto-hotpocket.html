Keto Hot Pocket

Made from fat head dough and filled with pepperoni, cheese, and pizza sauce. This Keto meal is so satisfying!

Whatâ€™s better than a flash back to your childhood. Hot pockets were a staple in my teenage years. This version is low-carb and still just as delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups mozzarella
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoings
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 25 pepperoni slices

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. In a microwave saft bowl, add the mozzarella cheese and cream cheese. Heat in 30 second intervals, stirring in between until fully melted.
  2. Add the almond flour, baking powder, Italian seasonings, and garlic salt. Stir until well combined.
  3. Roll out dough in between two parchment sheets to 1/4 inch roughly (about 10x14 inch rectangle). Cut into four equal parts.
  4. Brush half of each rectangle with about 1 tablespoon of marinara sauce, top with about 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese and top with about 5 pepperoni.
  5. Fold over and press edges together. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
